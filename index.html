A secluded woodland paradise, nestled deep in the redwood forest. At the edge of California's rugged Lost Coast, scenes of lush wilderness invigorate and rejuvenate the soul.
Facilities
Fully off-grid. Solar power with backup generator. Clean well water. Outdoor redwood shower. Wi-Fi. Indoor and outdoor kitchens. Fire pit. Shaded stage and dance floor. Professional sound system and DJ equipment.
Events
Parties, retreats, and weddings of up to 150 people. Camp beneath the stars, dance the night away, and commune with nature - without sacrificing creature comforts and amenities!
